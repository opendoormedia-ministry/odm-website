Your story is handled with care

We take what you share seriously. Here is exactly what happens after you hit send.

01

It comes to us privately

Your submission goes directly to the Open Door Media team. No one else sees it.

02

We read it with care

Every story gets read by a real person. We are not feeding anything into AI or automation.

03

We only publish with permission

The form asks directly. If you say no, your story stays private. Full stop.

04

We may reach out

If the story calls for a deeper interview or feature, we will ask first. You are never obligated.

Your email is used only to follow up if you said that is okay. We do not send newsletters, share your contact with anyone, or add you to any list.

Someone needs to hear your story.

✦

Someone else needs to hear it

Your story is not just yours. The thing God brought you through is the exact thing someone else is still in the middle of. Hearing that someone made it in their own words, with their own doubt and mess, can be the thing that keeps them holding on.

✦

It does not have to be dramatic

You do not need a before-and-after that would make a movie. Quiet faithfulness counts. A small moment where God showed up counts. If it mattered to you, it will matter to someone.

✦

Saying it out loud does something

There is something that happens when you put what God did into words. It becomes more real. More yours. Testimony is not just for the listener. It anchors the teller too.

✦

You are in control of what gets shared

You decide whether your story is published or stays private. You decide how much to share and what name to use. There is no pressure to be more public than you are comfortable with.
