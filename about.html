About Open Door Media

A small media team helping organizations do work that matters.

We're a mission-driven media team based in the Midwest. We partner with churches, nonprofits, and purpose-driven organizations to create websites, video, and marketing that help more people discover their work.

"We don't try to be Christians — we just are, and we try to solve real, everyday problems because that's what we're called to do."

Lucas Hafer · Founder & Creative Director

Who we serve

Is this your work?

If you're quietly making the world better, we want to help the right people find you.

Churches & ministries People looking for community, truth, and hope shouldn't have to search forever to find it.
Healthcare & direct primary care People deserve care that feels personal, accessible, and built around the whole person.
Mental health organizations When people are walking through difficult seasons, finding real support should be simple.
Family farms & local growers The people growing and providing good things for their communities deserve to be seen.
Homeschool communities & parent-led education Families investing in the next generation deserve resources, connection, and support that are easy to find.
Anti-trafficking organizations The people fighting exploitation, restoring lives, and creating freedom deserve every opportunity to be discovered.
Food co-ops & community organizations Strong communities are built when people can find the resources, relationships, and places that help them thrive.
Artists, makers & creatives Creative work shapes culture, carries ideas, and helps people see the world differently.
Small businesses with a purpose Good organizations doing meaningful work deserve the tools to reach the people they're trying to serve.
…and the overlooked Didn't see yourself above? We're still listening.
